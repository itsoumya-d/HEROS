## 2024-05-28 - Adding skip links and visible focus styles for accessibility
**Learning:** Adding a hidden-until-focused 'Skip to content' link is critical for keyboard accessibility, especially for users who navigate with the Tab key. Custom focus-visible styles also help significantly in dark themes where default browser focus outlines might not provide sufficient contrast.
**Action:** Always verify keyboard navigation and ensure there's a quick way to bypass navigation headers to jump to the main content. Ensure custom focus outlines are added, particularly for dark mode interfaces.
